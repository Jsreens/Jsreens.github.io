Weekly assignment #13: What does the price of Dogecoin reveal to us?

Over the past year we have seen an incredible serge of volume transfer over to the digital currency space for a wide variety of reasons. New investors are typically drawn to the promising advice offered by youtubers and other influencers. Cryptocurrency is an extremely Volatile market, and this is partially due to its unregulated nature and the pump and dump schemes that go hand-in-hand with these influencers. However so-called ’Meme Coins’such as Dogecoin, which was created to be a joke in response to the many alternate projects created using blockchain, has helped me realize the power of online communities in finance.
